Zill-e-Huma

A Visionary in Quranic Arabic Education

Zill-e-Huma's journey into Quranic studies began from a place of deep spiritual yearning. Her quest to connect with the Quran in its original language wasn't just about intellectual pursuit, but a personal journey of spiritual fulfillment. Over a decade of dedicated study, Zill-e-Huma immersed herself in the profound depths of the Quranic language, driven by a desire to unlock the timeless wisdom of the Quran for herself and others.

In 2018, recognizing the challenges many face when trying to access comprehensive and structured Quranic education, Zill-e-Huma began developing her own resources. What started as personal notes grew into a wide-ranging collection of educational tools, designed to simplify the complexities of Quranic Arabic. Her carefully crafted approach ensures that students not only learn the technical aspects of the language but also experience the spiritual depth and beauty that the Quran offers.

With a heart centered on serving the community and fostering growth, Zill-e- Huma founded Direction to Divine (DTD), which has become a trusted source for Quranic studies. Offering resources like the Amazed by Quran Linguistic Series, Prophet Stories, and free Arabic courses, DTD helps students of all backgrounds connect with the Quran in its purest form.

Through her unique blend of structured teaching, deep-rooted spiritual insight, and commitment to accessible education, Zill-e-Huma has impacted countless students. Her approach to Quranic language learning emphasizes not only understanding the words but also appreciating the eloquence and divine message embedded within each verse.

Today, Zill-e-Huma continues to inspire and lead students on their Quranic journeys, with a focus on both linguistic mastery and spiritual growth. Her work through DTD is a testament to her unwavering dedication to fostering a deeper connection to the Quran for all who seek it.

"When a flower struggles to bloom, we don't change the flower; we enrich its environment!

At DTD, my mission is to create a nurturing space where my students can flourish-embracing purposeful living, finding inner peace, and cultivating illuminated minds. In this Quranic journey together, we will grow and glow in every aspect of our lives."

Zill-e-Huma, Founder of Direction to Divine (DTD)
